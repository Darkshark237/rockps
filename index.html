This is the EULA for the RPS game

The Rock Paper Scissors is an easy game where you can play against the computer. The rules are simple
1 - Rock beats scissors
2 - scissors cuts paper (the paper's image is a leave in our case ... French people call it that way)
3 - paper beats rock (because it covers the rock)
In this specific game, you can just click on the image you want to bet and see if you win against what the computer has chosen in the background. The score track is maintain at the top corner right.
